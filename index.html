A Legacy

Why Beyoncé's Renaissance Is Culturally Monumental

Beyoncé's latest album, Rennaissance, has spawned global success and proves just how monumental and influential she is 25 years into her career. The album debuted July 29, 2022, with the lead single "Break My Soul" sending messages of liberation and unwavering self-expression. The album was carefully crafted with the LGBTQ community in mind, paying homage to some of our most significant influences, dead and alive.

Who Is Beyoncé

She Is An Icon.

A Mother.

A Role Model.

A Queen.

Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter, born on September 4, 1981, known commonly as Beyoncé, is an American-born Houston, Texas native with many achievements under her belt. Knowles rose to fame in the late 1990s as the lead singer of the R&B girl group Destiny's Child, one of the world's best-selling girl groups of all time.

Time Magazine has included Beyoncé on its list of the "100 Most Influential People in the World". Knowles has attained five Hot 100 number one singles as a solo performer and four with Destiny's Child, and as a solo artist. Selling over 35 million albums and singles in the U.S.; according to Sony, her total record sales, when combined with the group, have surpassed 100 million. On December 11, 2009, Billboard listed Knowles as the most successful female artist of the 2000s decade and the top Radio Artist of the decade. In February 2010, the RIAA listed her as the top certified artist of the decade.

Discography

Beyoncé has released seven studio albums, five live albums, three compilation albums, five EPs, one soundtrack album, two karaoke albums, and 83 singles (including 15 as a featured artist, 11 promotional singles and 6 charity singles). Beyoncé has sold over 200 million records worldwide as a solo artist and a further 60 million as part of Destiny's Child, making her one of the best-selling music artists of all time. Billboard ranked her as the 37th greatest artist of all time.

TV and Film Appearances

Beyoncé has appeared in many television shows and movies, such as Austin Powers, Obsessed, Fighting Temptations, and more. Her most notable movie appearances come from her arranged deals with major streaming platforms such as Disney, HBO, and Netflix. These deals pioneered a new direction for artists to showcase their work in a visual story format.
